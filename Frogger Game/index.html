Frogger Game

Instruction : Use arrow keys to reach to the destination and gain 1 point.

Collect items

  • Blue Gem - Bugs' speed decrease 5 second!
  • Green Gem - 2 points!
  • Orange Gem - 3 points!
  • Heart - one life!

Avoid items

  • Bug - lose one life!
  • Water - lose one life!
  • Rock - lose one life!
